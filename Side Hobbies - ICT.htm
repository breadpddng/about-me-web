Making Drinks

I really enjoy making drinks in my free time! I really enjoy things like bubble tea and coffee, so to both save money and explore my creativity, I like to make drinks and make different flavours.

Here's a recipe that I reccomend! It's pretty and tasteful :)

Strawberry Matcha Drink

    
 
  1. Milk (any kind)
    2. 
 
  2. Strawberry flavouring (Syrup, Strawberries, Powder, etc.)
    3. 
 
  3. Matcha powder
    4. 
 
  4. Green Tea
    5. 
 
  5. [optional] Ice
    6.

Steps!

Add the strawberry flavouring to any milk of your choice and mix it up in a cup. In another cup, Mix up the matcha powder with a bit of hot water while steeping the tea. Once you have your strawberry milk and matcha green tea, add each component into a cup (slowly) to create a green and pink masterpiece! To prevent it from mixing while pouring it in, add a layer of ice in between the two parts.

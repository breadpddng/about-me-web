I've always loved art from a young age, so I started taking lessons around 2nd grade.
I slowly established my own style in the summer of 6th grade with the help of my brother.
He was very supportive of me starting a new hobby :).

I started journaling last year. I do it around once a week, and it's nice to write my feelings down or just schedule the day ahead.

Both of my hobbies allow me to be creative. I love goofing around and drawing funny doodles or making messy collages,
but I also love creating more expressive and detail oriented portraits and schedules.
They are both ways that allow me to de-stress and relax.
